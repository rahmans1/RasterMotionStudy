Raster Motion Study

Sakib Rahman and Ciprian Gal

Rastered Case

Simulation Setting

  • 5 million events for moller and elastics each
  • 5x5 mm raster, 0.065 mrad/mm at target center
  • Configurations tested (Only horizontal offsets i.e. motion along x-axis):
    1. Beam Position Offsets: -2, -1, 0, 1, 2 mm
    2. Beam Angle Offsets: -1, -0.5, 0, 0.5, 1 mm at target center
  • Implementation has magnetic field and detector plane positions shifted by the small distances. These effects are ignored in analysis.
